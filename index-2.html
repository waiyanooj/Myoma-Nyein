Myoma Nyein (1909-1955)

He is a composer and musician who graduated from Alanka Kyawtha, who was prominent in the world of Myanmar music. In addition, he is one of the founders of Mandalay City's prestigious city entertainment instrument group, which is one of Myanmar's longest-running musical instrument associations.
